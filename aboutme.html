About Me:

Why the name 3200.DDR4?


My initials are R.A.M., when I was coming up for a name for my Photography instagram account I wanted something a little different than just 'Rob Mosher's Photography'. 3200.DDR4 was a mix of unique and a reference to my tech side, and I've kept it ever since!


In my free time, I love to run, cycle, ski, rock climb, backpack, and shoot photography! The pursuit of these passions has brought me all over the United States, and I'm always travelling somewhere new! My dream in life is to have a lifestyle that will let me explore and see the world as I age, not just when I'm older.
